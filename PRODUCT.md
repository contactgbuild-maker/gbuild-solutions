# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary users are SME owners, startup founders, operations leaders, and institutional decision-makers evaluating a technical partner for websites, platforms, AI automation, and business digitalization. They are usually not buying code alone; they are buying clarity, reliability, and an implementation partner who can turn business friction into a working system.

## Product Purpose

This website exists to position GBuild Solutions as a serious software, AI systems, web platform, and digitalization company. Its job is to explain the offer clearly, prove technical credibility, connect the brand to the broader Gardens of Babylon and GB Vertical ecosystem without confusing identities, and convert qualified visitors into discovery calls and project inquiries.

## Brand Personality

Professional, precise, grounded. The tone should feel premium without arrogance, technically advanced without hype, and human-made rather than AI-generated. It should communicate structured thinking, execution discipline, and calm confidence.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-agency templates with glowing gradients, robot imagery, and empty hype
- Neon cyberpunk aesthetics or sci-fi visual language
- Cheap startup-section scaffolding such as repetitive icon cards, fake stats, and vague buzzword heroes
- Editorial-serif monoculture that looks stylish but weakens trust for a software implementation company
- Decorative 3D that adds noise instead of meaning

## Design Principles

- Explain business value before showing technical depth.
- Make trust come from structure, specificity, and operational realism.
- Use design sophistication to signal engineering quality, not to perform trendiness.
- Keep every interaction intentional, polished, and performance-aware.
- Treat the site as a conversion system and brand proof, not just a visual brochure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA as the default quality bar. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, adequate contrast, reduced-motion alternatives, readable mobile layouts, and content that remains understandable for non-technical buyers. Founder-specific accessibility constraints remain To Verify.
