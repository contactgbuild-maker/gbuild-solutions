# Google Stitch Master Prompt - GBuild Solutions

Design a full responsive website UI for `gbuildsolutions.online`.

The brand is `GBuild Solutions`, a professional software, AI, and digital transformation company. It is part of a broader company ecosystem that includes `GB Vertical`, but this website must have its own identity as a serious software and AI solutions company. The design must feel premium, modern, trustworthy, and human-made.

## Core brand direction

- professional B2B software studio
- practical AI systems company
- digital transformation partner for SMEs, startups, and institutions
- calm, technical, trustworthy, and premium without arrogance
- human-designed, not AI-template looking

## Brand atmosphere

Create a restrained, premium, editorial-technical interface with clean spacing, strong typography, and subtle asymmetry. It should feel like a modern systems company that builds useful digital infrastructure, not a hype-heavy startup.

## Color direction

Use a light, calm background with one dominant accent color.

Preferred palette:

- background: `#F7F7F4`
- surfaces: `#FFFFFF`
- primary text: `#1D232B`
- secondary text: `#7D8793`
- borders: `#D7D3CB`
- accent / CTA: `#0E6B68`
- restrained detail tone: `#C9B79C`

Do not use purple neon, electric blue glow, or cyberpunk gradients.

## Typography direction

Use a premium sans-serif such as `Geist` or `Satoshi`.

Typography should feel:

- controlled
- modern
- readable
- business-grade

Do not use `Inter`.
Do not use decorative futuristic fonts.

## Design principles

- no emojis
- no AI cliche visuals
- no robot icons everywhere
- no glowing brains, network spheres, or sci-fi shapes
- no generic startup template feel
- no overloaded 3-card feature grid repeated across the site
- no fake stats or fake client logos
- no overly centered generic hero
- no visual noise

## Motion direction

Use subtle animations only:

- fade and rise section reveals
- soft staggered appearance of cards or timeline items
- tactile button hover states
- minor image or panel motion if useful

Keep motion tasteful and minimal.

## Layout direction

- use clean asymmetry in hero and key service sections
- strong spacing and clear content hierarchy
- generous whitespace
- professional grid structure
- text should never overlap visuals
- design for desktop first, then fully responsive mobile layouts

## Page list to design

Create UI concepts for:

- Home
- About
- Services
- AI Solutions
- Web & SaaS Development
- Portfolio / Case Studies
- Process
- Contact

## Homepage requirements

The homepage must include:

- a strong hero with a clear positioning headline
- short supporting paragraph
- one primary CTA and one secondary CTA
- a section showing the main service pillars
- a credibility section explaining why clients should trust the company
- a featured block introducing `GB Vertical` as a specialized AgriTech branch in the wider ecosystem
- a selected work / proof section
- a process snapshot
- a closing CTA

Suggested homepage tone:

- practical
- clear
- premium
- not hype-heavy

## About page requirements

The About page should explain:

- who GBuild Solutions is
- what kind of company it is becoming
- how it works
- its relationship to the wider ecosystem
- its philosophy around useful digital systems

Keep it clean and credible.

## Services page requirements

The Services page should cover:

- custom software development
- AI systems
- business digitalization
- websites and web platforms
- SaaS platforms
- internal business tools
- automation
- CRM and dashboards
- technical consulting

Present services in a structured and readable way, with strong hierarchy and concise explanation.

## AI Solutions page requirements

The AI Solutions page should feel grounded and business-useful, not futuristic.

Focus on:

- AI-powered workflows
- internal copilots
- document and information handling
- automation support
- decision support systems

Use diagrams or workflow visuals instead of AI cliche illustrations.

## Web & SaaS Development page requirements

This page should show:

- platform building capability
- internal tools
- SaaS product capability
- portals and dashboards
- scalable architecture mindset

Use interface-forward visuals, clean layouts, and product-style presentation.

## Portfolio / Case Studies page requirements

The case studies page should feel structured and real.

Use:

- project cards
- category tags
- challenge / solution framing
- room for screenshots or structured placeholders

Do not rely on fake metrics.

## Process page requirements

The Process page should show a clean progression:

- Discover
- Define
- Design
- Build
- Launch
- Improve

Make it feel reassuring and professional.

## Contact page requirements

The Contact page should include:

- a clean intro
- a professional contact form
- project-type selection
- short project summary field
- low-friction CTA

It should feel serious and conversion-focused, not like a generic contact template.

## Mobile requirements

- fully responsive
- clean mobile menu
- readable type hierarchy
- stacked layout with clear spacing
- no horizontal overflow
- touch-friendly CTA sizes

## Final visual target

The website should look like a premium B2B software agency and AI systems studio with real business credibility. It should feel more concrete than typical AI agencies, more refined than a generic web studio, and more approachable than an enterprise consultancy.
