# GBuild Solutions - Website Strategy

**Date:** 2026-06-26

## 1. Website purpose

The website must help visitors quickly understand:

- who GBuild Solutions is
- what it builds
- how it works
- why it is credible
- how it relates to the broader ecosystem without losing its own identity
- how to start a conversation

## 2. Core website objectives

- establish trust
- communicate services clearly
- convert qualified leads
- position GBuild Solutions as a serious software and AI partner
- create a scalable content structure for future growth

## 3. Recommended pages

- Home
- About
- Services
- AI Solutions
- Web & SaaS Development
- Business Digitalization
- Portfolio / Case Studies
- Process
- Blog / Insights
- Contact

## 4. Page strategy

## Home

**Goal**

Give immediate clarity, trust, and direction.

**Target audience**

- SME owners
- founders
- operations leads

- what we do
- featured service pillars
- why clients work with us

## 5. Navigation strategy

- keep the main nav short and legible
- put `Services` and `Process` in the main path
- keep `Contact` as a persistent CTA
- allow service detail pages to be linked from services cards and footer

## 6. Trust strategy

Trust should come from:

- clarity
- structure
- proof
- specificity

Not from fake numbers or invented scale.
