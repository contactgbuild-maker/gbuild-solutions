# GBuild Solutions - Site Copy Strategy

## Copy Goals

- explain what the company does fast
- make services understandable to non-technical buyers
- frame AI in business terms
- sound credible, not performative
- move visitors toward a conversation

## Tone Rules

- simple
- clear
- calm
- precise
- business-aware

## Messaging Priorities

1. what problem we solve
2. what kind of systems we build
3. who we build for
4. why the approach is trustworthy
5. what next step to take

## Content Guardrails

Avoid:

- “revolutionary”
- “unlock your potential”
- “cutting-edge solutions” unless justified by concrete evidence
- vague agency language
- long abstract paragraphs about innovation

## Content Structure Logic

- homepage: clarity, service overview, trust, proof, CTA
- service pages: problem, what we build, who it fits, process, CTA
- about: company identity, philosophy, ecosystem context, trust
- process: anxiety reduction and delivery clarity
- contact: low-friction serious inquiry
