# GBuild Solutions - Animation System

## Animation Principles

- motion must support hierarchy, not decorate emptiness
- interactions should feel fast and confident
- premium motion should come from timing, staging, and polish
- reduced-motion support is mandatory

## Recommended Motion Uses

- hero content reveal
- section fade and rise
- card hover depth and micro-scale
- process-section transitions
- CTA press feedback
- controlled page transitions if they remain fast

## Motion Rules

- prefer transform and opacity
- avoid long dramatic entrances on repeated UI
- keep hover motion subtle and tactile
- never hide critical content behind animation timing

## Review Influences

- `impeccable`: ruthless simplification and hierarchy discipline
- `emil-design-eng`: polished interaction timing and premium micro-feedback
- `gpt-taste`: avoid generic layouts, ensure cinematic section rhythm without overbuilding
