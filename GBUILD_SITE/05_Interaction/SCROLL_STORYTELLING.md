# GBuild Solutions - Scroll Storytelling

## Goal

Create a premium chaptered scrolling experience without turning the site into an animation demo.

## Recommended Scroll Moments

- hero to services transition
- systems capability chapter with staged reveals
- process section with step emphasis
- case-study or proof section with calm sequencing

## Recommended Patterns

- layered reveal of hero supporting content
- progressive service framing
- sticky process title with scrolling step content if implemented tastefully
- subtle dark-to-light or light-to-dark chapter transitions

## Avoid

- over-scrubbed page elements everywhere
- scroll hijacking that harms usability
- heavy pinned sections that make the site feel slow
