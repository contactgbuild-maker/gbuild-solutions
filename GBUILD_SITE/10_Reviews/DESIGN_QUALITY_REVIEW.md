# GBuild Solutions - Design Quality Review

## Review Inputs

- `impeccable` review principles
- `emil-design-eng` interaction and motion principles
- `gpt-taste` anti-generic design principles
- UX clarity review
- performance review
- brand consistency review

## What Feels Strong

- the strategy already points toward a serious, grounded software brand
- the hybrid light/dark recommendation supports both trust and premium feel
- the page architecture is broad enough for growth without overcomplicating V1
- AI is framed as practical workflow improvement, not hype

## What May Look Generic

- an all-light B2B layout with repetitive service cards
- over-reliance on standard agency hero formulas
- using shadcn-style defaults without strong brand editing
- treating motion as fade-in on every section

## What May Look AI-Generated

- random glowing gradients
- generic AI visual motifs
- repeated icon-card rows
- buzzword-heavy hero copy
- decorative 3D with no narrative function

## What Needs Refinement Before Coding

- final brand option approval
- hero composition and signature visual concept
- case-study proof strategy
- exact 3D scope
- final content tone and CTA wording

## Impeccable Review Principles To Enforce

- strict visual hierarchy
- remove anything unnecessary
- no mediocre interactions
- typography must carry authority and readability
- contrast, spacing, and polish must feel deliberate

## Emil Kowalski Style Principles To Enforce

- premium motion with strong easing discipline
- tactile press and hover feedback
- polished transform origins and entry states
- no sluggish repeated animations
- details should feel invisible but correct

## Taste Skill Principles To Enforce

- no generic templates
- no random gradients
- no clutter pretending to be sophistication
- strong aesthetic judgment in layout decisions
- each section should look chosen, not auto-generated

## UX Clarity Review

- messaging must be business-readable
- CTAs must be obvious but not pushy
- page hierarchy must support different buyer types
- contact flow must remain short and credible

## Performance Review

- keep 3D scoped and lazy-loaded
- use animation selectively
- watch mobile performance early
- prioritize image optimization and font discipline

## Brand Consistency Review

- maintain one coherent accent system
- use dark chapters intentionally
- keep service pages related but not repetitive
- align all copy with grounded, premium positioning

## Design Principles To Enforce During Implementation

- clarity before spectacle
- proof before hype
- polish before quantity
- interaction quality over animation quantity
- one strong visual idea per section
