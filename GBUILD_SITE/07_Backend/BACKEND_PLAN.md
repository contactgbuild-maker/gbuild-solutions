# GBuild Solutions - Backend Plan

## V1 Decision

V1 does **not** need a heavy backend.

## Recommended V1

- static-first marketing site
- contact form route handler
- spam protection
- email notification
- optional lead persistence

## Why

- faster launch
- simpler maintenance
- enough for real lead capture
- leaves room for future growth without premature complexity

## Future Backend Scope

- blog/content management
- case-study management
- newsletter
- lead pipeline tracking
- admin dashboard
- client portal later
