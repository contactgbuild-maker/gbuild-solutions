# GBuild Solutions - User Journeys

## Journey 1 - SME Looking For A Better Website Or Internal System

1. lands on homepage
2. understands the company builds business-useful digital systems
3. moves to Services or Business Digitalization
4. sees process clarity and trust signals
5. submits contact form or requests discovery call

## Journey 2 - Startup Founder Looking For MVP Or SaaS Partner

1. enters via homepage or Web & SaaS Development page
2. validates product and engineering credibility
3. reviews case studies or process
4. books discovery call

## Journey 3 - Company Exploring AI Automation

1. enters via AI Solutions page
2. sees practical AI use cases
3. understands the company avoids hype and builds grounded workflows
4. starts conversation through AI-focused CTA

## Journey 4 - Institution Validating Credibility

1. visits homepage and About
2. checks company story, process, and proof
3. reviews service structure and delivery seriousness
4. uses Contact or Request a quote path
