# Design System: GBuild Solutions

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

A restrained, premium B2B software-studio interface with editorial calm, business credibility, and confident asymmetry. The atmosphere should feel like a modern systems company rather than a trend-chasing AI startup. Density is balanced, motion is subtle, and hierarchy is driven by spacing, typography, and structural rhythm rather than visual noise.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- **Cloud White** (`#F7F7F4`) - primary background surface
- **Pure Surface** (`#FFFFFF`) - inner content containers
- **Graphite Ink** (`#1D232B`) - primary text and dark surfaces
- **Slate Mist** (`#7D8793`) - secondary text and metadata
- **Stone Line** (`#D7D3CB`) - borders and structural dividers
- **Deep Teal Accent** (`#0E6B68`) - primary CTA, links, focus ring, active states
- **Warm Sand Detail** (`#C9B79C`) - restrained supporting detail, never a second primary accent

Rules:

- one dominant accent only: `Deep Teal`
- no purple or blue-neon accents
- no pure black
- no glowing gradients

## 3. Typography Rules

- **Display:** `Geist` or `Satoshi` - track-tight, controlled, confident
- **Body:** `Geist` or `Satoshi` - readable, calm, 65ch max line length
- **Mono:** `JetBrains Mono` or `Geist Mono` - metadata, labels, technical references

Banned:

- `Inter`
- generic serif fonts
- oversized startup headline theatrics

## 4. Component Stylings

- **Buttons:** flat and tactile, slight press feedback, no glow
- **Cards:** lightly rounded, thin structured border, used selectively
- **Inputs:** label above field, clean focus state in `Deep Teal`
- **Navigation:** compact, professional, strong spacing
- **Case study blocks:** text-first with optional screenshot, not gallery noise
- **Service cards:** asymmetrical arrangement, never 3 identical equal-width tiles as the default pattern

## 5. Layout Principles

- max-width containment with generous horizontal breathing room
- asymmetry in hero and key service sections
- clear single-column collapse on mobile
- section spacing driven by rhythm, not filler
- grid-based layout over gimmicks
- no overlapping decorative layers

## 6. Motion & Interaction

- subtle fade-and-rise reveals
- small staggered content entrance
- tactile button hover/active states
- only transform and opacity-based motion
- respect reduced-motion preferences

## 7. Anti-Patterns (Banned)

- no emojis
- no neon cyberpunk visuals
- no robot heads or glowing AI brains
- no generic SaaS gradient heroes
- no glassmorphism overload
- no 3-column equal-card feature farms
- no fake stats
- no buzzword-heavy hero copy
- no "scroll to explore" filler text
